Unicol S.r.l.

Unicol S.r.l. is an Italian company that has been producing industrial adhesives since 1977. The company offers a vast range of products capable of satisfying multiple requests from various industrial sectors such as wood processing, panels, construction, textile, and tissue. With over 45 years of experience, Unicol has distribution in many countries and offers a wide catalogue of high-quality bonding solutions.
